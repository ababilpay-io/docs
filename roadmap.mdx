## Product phases

| Phase | Status | Scope |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Phase 1: Wallet & Payments Foundation | Complete | Merchant wallet provisioning, testnet USDC setup, balance sync, basic deposit and transfer flow, transaction history, and webhook handling. |
| Phase 2: Payment MVP | Complete | Hosted payment intent, merchant payment link, customer testnet checkout, payment status tracking, and basic merchant dashboard. |
| Phase 3: Merchant Tools | In progress | Invoice creation, sending, and payment flow; refund support; transaction history; API key management; webhook subscriptions; developer quickstart docs. |
| Phase 4: Cross-chain & Automation | Upcoming | Cross-chain bridge, auto-convert, AI invoice reminders, fraud detection, bridge route optimization, and operational audit logs. |
| Phase 5: Public Testnet Launch | Planned | Public testnet docs, sample apps, known limitations guide, and full feedback program. |

## Future capabilities

| Feature | Description | Phase |
| --- | --- | --- |
| USDC yield | Idle USDC earns yield through integrated lending protocols. | 4 |
| QR POS payments | Physical store checkout by QR scan. | 4 |
| AI expense categories | Agent tags transactions automatically for merchant accounting. | 3 |
| Subscription engine | Recurring billing with auto-charge for SaaS and subscription merchants. | 3 |
| White-label SDK | Embed AbabilPay under your own brand — for fintechs and platforms. | 3 |
| Carbon offset | Optional micro-donation per transaction for ESG-conscious merchants. | 4 |
| Loyalty points | Consumers earn fee-discount points for paying with AbabilPay. | 4 |
| AI price recommendations | Agent suggests invoice pricing based on market and customer history. | 4 |

## One-line pitch

AbabilPay is Stripe for the crypto economy: USDC-native, multichain, low-volatility for merchants, and powered by an AI agent layer that automates payment operations.
